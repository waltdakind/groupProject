Here at TrendGetter, our goal is simple: keep you informed on what's trending!


To use TrendGetter, use the arrows on either side of the page to cycle through images of the latest trending topics according to Twitter. When you see a topic you want to stay informed on, click the arrow underneath that image to Learn More !
